Why Volunteer With WHEA?

Volunteering is both a selfless and rewarding way to support a cause you care about. While there are a million reasons why you should volunteer with WHEA, here are our top 3 reasons:

  • Your volunteering experience will help you develop invaluable professional skills and build your leadership capabilities.
  • No matter who you are or where you are, we have a wide range of volunteering options for you.
